Jusoor Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Summary: Jusoor processes camera data on your device for sign language recognition. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Jusoor ("we," "our," or "us"). Jusoor is a sign language recognition application designed to bridge communication. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Our commitment: Every sign matters, Every word connects - and so does your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Camera Data

Jusoor requires camera access to recognize sign language gestures. This data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never stored or recorded
  • Used only for real-time sign recognition

Local Storage

We may store the following on your device:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Learning progress (if applicable)

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Photos or videos
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Usage analytics that identify you

4. How We Use Information

Camera input is used solely for:

  • Real-time sign language gesture recognition
  • Converting signs to text within the app

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any user data.

6. Data Security

Since all processing happens on your device and no data is transmitted externally, your information remains secure on your own device.

7. Children's Privacy

Jusoor is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

8. Third-Party Services

Jusoor may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (Android) - for app distribution
  • Apple App Store (iOS) - for app distribution

These services have their own privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored preferences.
